Color Twist - Privacy Policy

**Color Twist Privacy Policy**
This privacy policy explains how Color Twist, a game developed by Theophil Ronald, handles your personal data and respects your privacy.
Color Twist does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data from you or your device. The game does not require any permissions, access, or internet connection to function. It does not use any third-party services, libraries, or frameworks that may collect or process your personal data. Additionally, Color Twist does not display any ads, offer any in-app purchases, or link to any external websites or social media platforms.
The only data that the game stores locally on your device are your game settings, preferences, scores, and achievements. This data is solely used to enhance your gaming experience and is not accessible by anyone else. You have the option to delete this data at any time by uninstalling the game from your device.
Color Twist is committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a safe and enjoyable gaming experience. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at ronaldtdas@gmail.com.
